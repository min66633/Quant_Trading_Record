This repository is a real-time validation log of a rule-based, risk-managed systematic trading strategy, conducted after out-of-sample testing.
Please note the following when reviewing the results:

Focus on risk management, not headline returns. The strategy targets leveraged ETFs and is currently in a paper-trading phase during a strong market period. As such, the absolute return figures are amplified and should not be read as the main achievement. The intended focus is drawdown control and risk-adjusted performance.
Drawdown control vs. benchmark. While the benchmark experienced a maximum drawdown of roughly 40–50% over the same period, this strategy contained its maximum drawdown to around 15%.
Execution costs. Because the strategy holds positions over multi-day periods with low turnover, the impact of slippage and commissions is designed to be limited rather than a primary driver of results.
